Faculty Panel
McGill TechWeek invites you to join us for a faculty panel discussion on engineering challenges, life post-COVID and more! The panel will consist of distinguished McGill Engineering professors across different disciplines who can share with you their diverse perspectives on real-world issues. If you are eager to learn about the state of academia or industry in engineering-related fields, or to obtain advice on what to do after graduation, this would be the event you want to join! As the opening event for TechWeek 2021, Dean Jim Nicell will give an opening speech.

Prof. Jim Nicell

Professors & Dean of Engineering, Ph.D., P.ENG

Professor James "Jim" Nicell is a registered professional engineer with formal training as both a chemical and environmental engineer. His research, teaching, administrative and consulting activities are diverse. The main focus of his research is on the development of enzymatic processes for the treatment of recalcitrant pollutants including industrial contaminants, endocrine disruptors, pharmaceuticals and other contaminants of emerging concern.
